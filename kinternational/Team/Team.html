Teams : Roles & Heads

Riddhima Pandey

Coordinators

As the dedicated coordinators of IIT Jammu's Kritash Club, our unwavering mission is to cultivate a pervasive culture of social responsibility and active community engagement within our esteemed institution. We wholeheartedly believe in the profound transformative potential that resides in collective efforts, and our primary aim is to channel the boundless enthusiasm and talents of our fellow students towards purposeful and impactful social welfare endeavours

Ayush Raj

Om Chaudhary

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Dedicated to showcase the events and activities going on in Kritash, our website is the first social handle to discover the club. Our website helps us reach out to more people and serve humanity. Realising the importance of the website, Kritash has its dedicated web development team, currently headed by Om Chaudhary and Aryan Katiyar. Web development team has complete rights over the website contents and ensures smooth functioning of the website.

Aryan Katiyar

Yash Bhajbhuje

MEDIA & MARKETING

Kritash's dedicated media and marketing team, led by Ankita Soni and Yash Bhajbhuje, ensures comprehensive event coverage through video and photo capture. They regularly upload these materials to Kritash's official website and social media channels. "Eye On Social," the official Kritash YouTube page, features bi-weekly videos covering recent events and gathering student opinions.

Ankita Soni

Priyanka

Aksharshala

Teaching is the biggest virtue a person can exercise in life. Following the same, we at Kritash have our Aksharshala team currently headed by Devesh Sharma and Priyanka. We not only provide education to the needy and underprivileged children but we also arrange various camps providing clothes, books, blankets etc. and medical camps for the needful people.

Devesh

Aaditya Sinha

DESIGN

Designing makes things creative and artistic. Kritash too has a design team led by Amber Kumar and Aaditya Sinha. The team is aimed to transform ideas into impactful posters which helps Kritash attract people for their involvement in different events. The team is also responsible for making presentations which helps our club to lay down ideas in front of our sponsors. Designing plays an important role in Kritash as designers have the potential to expand Kritash by combining their creativity and the knowledge of their design principles.

Amber Kumar

Vedika Aggarwal

EVENT MANAGEMENT

The event management team of Kritash headed by Rohan Ohri and Vedika Aggarwal, constitutes a dynamic group of passionate individuals dedicated to organise and manage memorable and impactful events. With a flair of creativity and passion for philanthropy , we orchestrate a wide range of activities, from donation drives to moral and cultural extravaganzas, leaving a lasting mark on the campus community

Rohan Ohri

Kashvi Bansal

SPONSORSHIP & OUTREACH

Kritash's Sponsorship and Outreach Team, under Prabhkirat Kaur and Kashvi Bansal's leadership, combines dedication and social responsibility. Their mission is to secure sponsorships for club activities, forging impactful partnerships to support education, healthcare, and more.

Prabhkirat Kaur
